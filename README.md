### 🧭 About Me

I'm a Software Engineering student who designs systems, builds them end-to-end, and then tries to break them — ethically — to understand what makes them strong.

My work sits at the intersection of three disciplines that most people keep separate:

- **Full-Stack & Software Engineering** — designing and shipping production-grade web apps and APIs with clean architecture.
- **AI & Machine Learning** — building agentic systems, fine-tuning models (including low-resource languages like Amharic and Afaan Oromo), and designing RAG pipelines that actually solve problems.
- **Offensive Security** — thinking like an attacker to design systems that don't fall over, through CTFs, web pentesting, and secure-by-design development.

Currently an **AI & Software Development Intern**, working on real systems that combine automation, agentic AI, and solid backend engineering.

I care less about "using a tool" and more about **why a system is designed the way it is** — trade-offs, failure modes, and how architecture decisions ripple downstream. That's the lens I bring to every project.
